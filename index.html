General Questions

  • What was your thesis about and what was the result?
  • What would you like to work?
  • If two people have conflicting opinions, how would you go about resolving this issue?
  • How many credit cards are issues each year in the country?
  • Why did you apply for this position?
  • What experiences do you have, that are relevant to this role?
  • Tell us something about you, that is not in your CV.
  • Tell us about an incident, in which you dealt with a situation in an innovative way.
  • Tell us about an incident where you faced uncertainty but had to remain productive. How did you remain productive?
  • If you had all the resources what kind of app would you develop?
  • What else would you like to tell us to help us hire you?
  • If lying was okay, what would you say to us to get this job?
